Explore Doctores and Roma Neighborhoods with street art and delicious food.


Perfect for art lovers, foodies, and curious travelers, this tour dives into the vibrant culture of two iconic neighborhoods. Discover the blend of local traditions and European architecture that shaped these areas in the late 19th century.
Walk through Doctores to see an impressive collection of 20th-century toys and modern objects, along with its thriving street art scene. Then, explore Roma’s beautiful parks and architecture while enjoying delicious Mexican street food.
A fun-filled route full of art, culture, and flavor!

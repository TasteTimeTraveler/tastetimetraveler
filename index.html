Recognized as the Best Urban Park in the World by the World Association of Urban Parks, inside the park is Chapultepec Castle, which was an imperial and presidential residence and the scene of important historical moments, exhibits an extensive art collection and offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city.

Discover Chapultepec History + Ticket Museum of Anthropology

Site of great historical, cultural and natural wealth. It houses one of the most important and impressive museums in the country and the world. Among its pieces is the famous Stone of the Sun or Aztec Calendar, an emblematic sculpture of the Aztec civilization, which offers a profound vision of its cosmovision and astronomical knowledge.

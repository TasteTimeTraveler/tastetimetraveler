Unearth the History and Culture of Mexico City on Foot

At Taste and Time Traveler, we invite you to discover Mexico City through our walking tours, where history, culture, and authentic flavors come alive. Accompanied by our passionate guides, you'll explore secret corners, iconic monuments, and energetic neighborhoods, while being amazed by panoramic views that reveal the city's grandeur from unique perspectives.

Whether it's your first time in Mexico or you're a seasoned traveler, our tours will offer you an unforgettable and fresh experience, giving you a deep connection to the magic of this vibrant metropolis. Book now and walk with us in the heart of Mexico City!

Our Experiences

Walking tour of the historic center of Mexico City

You will explore iconic landmarks, learn about the city's rich past and enjoy authentic flavors of Mexican cuisine. This immersive experience offers a unique insight into the heart of the city.

Discover the Guadalupan Codex and the Culture of this Sacred Site

One of the most important shrines of the Catholic Church and one of the most visited pilgrimage centers in the world. It is a place of deep spiritual significance. Here you can witness the impressive mix of devotion, history and culture.

A Royal Experience: Gardens and Views of Chapultepec Castle and Forest

Recognized as the Best Urban Park in the World, Chapultepec Castle was once an imperial and presidential residence, witnessing key historical moments and offering breathtaking city views.

Aztec Culture Unveiled: The Museum of Anthropology and Chapultepec Park

Site of great historical, cultural and natural wealth. This museum houses the famous Stone of the Sun, also known as the Aztec Calendar, an iconic artifact of Aztec civilization.
